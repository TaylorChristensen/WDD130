Services

Bookkeeping and Payroll – We will make sure to take care of all of your internal accounting needs from an external position. This will help you have more time to spend on running your business while having the peace of mind that your books will be professionally taken care of. We strive to provide this service with a focus on accuracy and timeliness in order to allow you to make the best decisions possible.

Financial Analysis – With this service we place an emphasis on getting everyone the access to CFO quality analysis for a great price. We will look into your Companies Financial Details and help you determine company health, Future projections, as well as provide our recommendations on where you can avoid financial ‘leakage’.

Tax Planning – This service can and should be utilized by both individuals and families in order to have the highest level of confidence in their long-term plan when it comes to taxes. We encourage everyone to plan now so that they can enjoy life freely later.

Tax Preparation (Individuals) – We strive to bring the maximum return to individuals in any income bracket. We know many tips and tricks to raise the tax savings/ refunds for individuals returns.

Tax Preparation (Business) – We are committed to help businesses save in taxes to help them maximize their impact on their customers and local areas. We want to give our clients the best chance to experience high growth through tax savings.

About Us

2022 Mission Statement - At Christensen Financial we strive to provide high quality Accounting services to ALL individuals and businesses. We do so by breaking the accounting process out into key service offerings; and by doing so we are able to provide an affordable service without lowering our quality. We have a goal to bring more good into the world by empowering those who have not felt support in the past.

Founder - Taylor Christensen, CPA Taylor is hardworking and dedicated and his focus always falls customer first. With his 7+ years in customer service alongside knowledge in accounting his main goal is to bring everyone the best accounting services at the best price. Taylor strongly believes that everyone has something to give to the world and wants to do his part in supporting anyone in their path to success.
