Overview

Purpose

Tired of overpaying for your Accounting services? At Christensen Financial we focus on providing you with the accounting services you NEED. We won't force you to pay for any unutilized services within any product package. Use our product catalogue to browse our services, build your personalized package, and receive high-quality service.

Audience

Our main target audience will be individuals, families and businesses who are in the lower to middle class. We will also target those in the age range of 20-55 years age ( due to the fact that this age is most comfortable with the internet.) An emphaisis will also be placed on the income bracket of $30,000 - $100,000 (Individuals and Families) and $$40,000 - $1,000,000 (Businesses).
