2022 Mission Statement

At Christensen Financial we strive to provide high quality Accounting services to ALL individuals and businesses. We do so by breaking the accounting process out into key service offerings; and by doing so we are able to provide an affordable service without lowering our quality. We have a goal to bring more good into the world by empowering those who have not felt support in the past.

2022 Impact Plan

Christensen Financial is determined to have an impact on the many local communities that it serves. We hace set our focus on having an impact 3 specific aspects of each community. Financial Education, Available Greenspace, and the Local Economy.
